When I am not working on improving my skills and learning more on my path to become a developer, I enjoy a few passtimes!

Doing Maintenance On My Truck

I enjoy working on vehicles and doing maintenance myself; my current vehicle, I do all of the work on by myself. Although it may not be too complex, it is something I enjoy doing it as it allows me to see what's actually going on with my vehicle and it is fun to me to see all the different working parts and how they work together. Sometimes it can be really complicated but a good challenge is always fun to problem solve with!

Modifying Ninento Switches

Uniqueness and style are important to me, so I enjoy modifying Nintendo Switches for myself and for my friends as well. As long as they supply the switch and the new casing they want to install I do all the work. Ive modified two regular Nintendo Switches and a Nintendo Switch Lite. It is really fun to take them apart and just seeing all of the different work parts as they come together to create this portable gaming console!

Collecting Funko Pop!'s

I have a small collection of Funko Pops that I have been collecting for the past few years. Although I may have 74 Pops, I only collect ones that I want to and I don't typically just go based off of resale value. About 70% of my collection is Star Wars Funko Pops, and some of my favorite Pops I have are my Stranger Things: Eddie Munson (1250) Pop, my Star Wars: The Ronin (505) Pop, and my Demon Slayer: Inosuke Hashibira (1090 Special Edition) Pop!
